Holly and Phil down shots with Rich Astley on ITV This Morning

It seems like it’s never too early for an alcoholic beverage on This Morning - after all, it’s 8pm somewhere in the world.Whether it be a cheeky cocktail or a splash of wine in a meal prepared by a chef, the show’s hosts Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield are always happy to partake when necessary.

Today was no different for the duo as they downed a shot of Jägermeister with Rick Astley, who was on the sofa tasking about his greatest hits album.The Never Gonna Give You Up hitmaker said that drinking the German herbal liqueur was part of a ‘ritual’, adding that part of the tradition is making everyone else in the room join in.
